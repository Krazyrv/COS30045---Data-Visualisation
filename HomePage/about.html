About our Project

Toan Khanh Nguyen - 104180605
Davy Sung - 103535521

This project will apply a structured iterative design process to research design and build an interactive data visualisation and present it on a website. The website is related to the project topic which is Economic on Life Expectancy.

We designed and developed a variety of visualizations to help us understand the context and patterns of life expectancy in relation to GDP. These visual tools are instrumental in uncovering patterns and trends that may not be immediately evident through raw data alone. By employing different types of charts and graphs, such as choropleth maps, density chart and scatter plots we aim to provide a comprehensive and nuanced view of how GDP influences life expectancy.

This approach allows us to capture a comprehensive view of the dynamics at play, identify significant insights, and inform policy decisions, guiding further research and contributing to a broader understanding of the socio-economic determinants of health. Through clear and compelling representations, we aim to provoke thought and discussion among policymakers, researchers, and the public, ultimately bridging the gap between data and decision-making to improve life expectancy and overall well-being.
